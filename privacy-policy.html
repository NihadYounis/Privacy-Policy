Privacy Policy for Terreno

Effective Date: 2026-04-29

Terreno ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We only collect the information necessary to provide our services:

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data.

We may share data only if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Third-Party Services

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

Contact us to make these requests.

You can delete your account by contacting support. This action is permanent.
